Crystal Collector

The four crystals below are the four types of "soul gems" you have been assigned to collect. Every game starts a new day, and the market values of the gems fluctuate each day. You will be assigned a quota each day; your goal is to collect crystals to match that quota in gold points. You will not know the value of each crystal until you collect it; they are valued 1-12. If you go your quota, the boss will think you cheated, and you will not get paid that day. Good luck!
